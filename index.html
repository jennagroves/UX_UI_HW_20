You May Be Asking:
Why Fall?

Let me paint the picture for you

Leaves crunching, crisp air, the smell of apples and pumpkin with cinnamon in the air, hazy fog in the morning, boots with tights, scarves, hot coffee, dark nail polish, fireplaces, Halloween, Harry Potter, scary movies. I could go on and on about all the things that make fall so wonderful. Not ony that, but after fall, the next best season is here: winter! The end of the year when everything is changing and transforming to become anew in the spring. The transitional period that is fall is always so breathtaking and calming when you really get the chance to breathe it in and absorb it.
